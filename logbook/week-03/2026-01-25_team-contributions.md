### Reflection

Overall, I believe the team was able to create an excellent report which met all rubric requirements with immense detail, clarity, and cohesion. The report was
structured, and the use of flowcharts to explain the implementation of each subsystem made the proposed technical approach very clear and easy to understand. Going
forward, I believe it is valuable to have team review sessions (like we did for this report), since it helps identify areas of improvement and encourages meaningful
discussions, which improve the quality of the report. Since the team was struggling with converting the report to .PDF format when submitting and had to email the
professor about this technical issue, it is best that the team does not make significant formatting edits (like updating table of contents or figures) when a lot
of people are editing the report, as we believe this was the reason that the file could not be converted (proposed changes were potentially backlogged).
